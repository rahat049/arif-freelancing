Management Tools

As a freelancer, we use few effective management tools managing our projects efficiently and is paramount to team/our success. Thankfully, numerous tools exist to streamline your workflow and enhance productivity. Here are some management tools tailored for our freelancing works:
1. Trello: Trello is a versatile project management tool that uses boards, lists, and cards to organize tasks. We create boards for different projects and use cards to track tasks, deadlines, and progress. Its intuitive interface makes it easy to collaborate with clients or team members.
2. Asana: Asana is another popular project management tool that allows to break-down our projects into tasks, you can set deadlines, and assign us to team members or yourself. Its visual layout and customizable features make it suitable for freelancers of all types.
3. Todoist: Todoist is a simple yet powerful task management tool that lets us/you create to-do lists, set priorities, and schedule deadlines. Its minimalist design and cross-platform compatibility make it a favorite among freelancers who value simplicity and efficiency.
4. Slack: Communication is key in freelancing, and Slack provides a platform for seamless communication with clients and team members. Possible to create channels for different projects, share files, and integrate with other tools like Trello or Asana for better project management.
5. Clockify /Harvest: Tracking your time is essential for billing accurately and understanding your productivity patterns. Harvest is a time tracking tool that allows you to log hours, generate invoices, and analyze your time usage. It integrates with popular project management tools, making it easy to track time spent on specific tasks.
6. Google Workspace (formerly G Suite): Google Workspace offers a suite of productivity tools including Gmail, Google Drive, Google Docs, Sheets, and Calendar. These tools are invaluable for collaboration, document sharing, and scheduling meetings with clients or team members.
7. Notion: Notion is an all-in-one workspace that combines note-taking, task management, and collaboration features. It allows you to create custom workflows, databases, and wikis, making it highly adaptable to different freelancing needs.
